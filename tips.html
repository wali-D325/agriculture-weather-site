🧑‍🌾 Farming Tips

  • 💧 Water your crops early in the morning or late in the evening to reduce evaporation.
  • 🌿 Rotate crops each season to maintain soil fertility and reduce pests.
  • 🪱 Use compost and organic fertilizers to enrich your soil.
  • 🧪 Test your soil before planting to understand nutrient needs.
  • 🌾 Choose crops suitable for your climate and soil type.
  • 🧹 Keep your field clean to reduce weed growth and diseases.
  • 🐞 Encourage beneficial insects like ladybugs for natural pest control.
  • 📅 Follow local weather reports to plan irrigation and planting schedules.
